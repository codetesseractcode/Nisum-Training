Light/Dark Mode Toggle

About Light/Dark Mode

Light and dark modes are two color schemes that can be chosen to display a website or app.

Light mode uses a light background with dark text, which is the traditional look for many websites.

Dark mode uses a dark background with light text, which some users prefer for reducing eye strain, especially in low-light conditions.

Benefits of Dark Mode

  • Reduces eye strain in low-light conditions
  • Can save battery power on OLED/AMOLED screens
  • Creates a sleek, modern aesthetic
  • May be preferred by users working at night

Benefits of Light Mode

  • Better readability in bright lighting conditions
  • Traditional look that many users are accustomed to
  • Often provides better contrast for reading long content
  • May be preferred for daytime use
